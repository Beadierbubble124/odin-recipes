Rocky Road (Ice Cream)

Description

Cold treat for a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • Condensed milk
  • Cocoa powder
  • Heavy cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chopped pecans
  • Marshmallows

Steps

      Step 1: Stir the condensed milk and cocoa powder in a suacepan until smooth
      Step 2: Put mixture into icecream maker untill frozen
      Step 3(Optional): Add pecan and marshallows as toppings
